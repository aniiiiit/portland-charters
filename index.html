Our Story

Born from a Love of the Sea

After watching the family boat tied to a mooring for a couple of summers, Captain Larus ran into a hotelier in Portland who asked if he could take his guests out on the water. The idea had been brewing for months -- and the business took off.

With a USCG Captain's License and over four decades of sailing experience from Iceland to Maine, Captain Larus brings unmatched knowledge of these waters to every voyage.
