The Draco

The Draco is a classic Scandinavian-designed boat built for the waters of the North Atlantic. With its deep navy hull, red leather interior, and clean lines, it turns heads at every marina.

Originally designed in Norway, the Draco combines timeless Nordic craftsmanship with reliable performance. Perfect for cruising the usually calm waters of Casco Bay in style.

The Gemma

The Gemma is a 2005 Gorbon 34, cold-molded wood epoxy built in Istanbul. With a Herreshoff-style mahogany interior, teak cockpit, and a 440 hp Yanmar turbo diesel, she combines old-world craftsmanship with serious performance.

USCG documented and built to handle the waters of the North Atlantic, the Gemma is a true ocean-going vessel with the character and warmth of a classic yacht.
